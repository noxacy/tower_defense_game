About Handn Games

Welcome to Huvyu Games, an independent game development hub dedicated to bringing high-quality, browser-based gaming experiences to players worldwide. Founded in 2026, our mission is to push the boundaries of what is possible with web technologies like WebAssembly and Python.

Our Vision

We believe that gaming should be accessible to everyone, everywhere, without the need for high-end hardware or complex installations. By leveraging the power of the Pygbag compiler and Pygame, we transform native desktop-grade code into smooth, responsive web applications.

The Developers

Noxacy and Huzeyfe are a duo of passionate developers focused on systems programming, game mechanics, and web optimization. From complex Tower Defense strategies to fast-paced arcade shooters, we handle everything from engine logic to user interface design. We are currently operating as a small indie studio, focusing on community feedback to improve our gameplay loops and performance.

Technology

We utilize Pygbag to translate our Python code into WebAssembly, allowing our games to run natively in your browser without any plugins.
